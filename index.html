Springtime

In the gentle bloom of spring's embrace,
Nature's canvas painted with grace,
Whispers of blossoms fill the air,
A symphony of life, beyond compare.

Each petal a verse in beauty's tale,
In vibrant hues, the soul prevails,
With every bud, a promise anew,
Of dreams unfurling, pure and true.

As sunlight dances on dew-kissed leaves,
And fragrant breezes weave through eaves,
The world awakes from winter's slumber,
In springtime's embrace, hearts remember.

Oh, poet of spring, with words unspoken,
In nature's song, our spirits awoken,
In this season of renewal and delight,
Let us dance with joy in nature's light.
